Live Site https://covidsafepaths.org

Summary

COVID Safe Paths (based on Private Kit) is an open and privacy preserving system to use personal information to battle COVID. It is a collaboration between MIT, COVID Watch and CoEPi, and has been endorsed for public use by Connecticut Governor Ned Lamont, and the jurisdictions of Hawaii, Guam, Puerto Rico, and Cyprus.
